All communications between you and our firm regarding legal representation are protected by attorney-client privilege and the ethical duty of confidentiality. We maintain the highest standards of confidentiality in accordance with the Rules of Professional Conduct in California, Texas, New York, and the American Bar Association Model Rules.

Important: Do not send confidential or sensitive information through unsecured email or website contact forms until an attorney-client relationship has been formally established.
