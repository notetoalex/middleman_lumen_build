About Lumen IP Law

Lumen IP Law is a solo practice by Alexander Lee, J.D., Ph.D., an attorney licensed to practice law in California, New York, and Texas, as well as before the United States Patent and Trademark Office.

Prior to founding Lumen IP Law, Mr. Lee worked at several prominent law firms where he honed his skills in intellectual property law. Having worked in both patent prosecution and litigation, he brings a well-rounded perspective to his practice.

Technical Fluency Drives Better Outcomes

Mr. Lee is fluent in broad areas of science and technology as a result of having degrees in biochemistry, chemical engineering, and electrical engineering. As a result, he is able to comprehend complex technical issues and communicate effectively with clients as well as other professionals working in the field of intellectual property.

Litigation-Informed Patent Strategy

Experience of working in patent litigation fundamentally changes how patents are prosecuted, as issues of validity and enforcement shapes how patents and drafted and prosecuted. Litigation-informed prosecution delivers client-focused strategies that enhance the strength and enforceability of patents.
