With a unique blend of technical expertise and legal acumen, Mr. Lee brings comprehensive understanding of complex intellectual property matters to the practice. Prior to his legal career, he worked in the pharmaceutical and high-tech industries in Silicon Valley and Santa Barbara, where he gained firsthand experience with cutting-edge technology and innovation processes that drive today's most dynamic industries.

This technical foundation led Mr. Lee to transition into the legal field, where he served as a science advisor at a prominent patent litigation firm. In this role, he developed deep insights into the intersection of technology and law, working closely with attorneys to analyze complex technical disputes and provide technical guidance on patent matters.

While attending law school, Mr. Lee further expanded his experience by prosecuting patents. This valuable hands-on experience gave him a solid foundation in patent prosecution.

Outside his practice, Mr. Lee enjoys classical guitar, web application development, and biking.
